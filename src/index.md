## About University Libraries

The University of Iowa Libraries is a congressionally designated depository for U.S. Government information. We provide comprehensive research support, instruction, and access to millions of print and electronic resources to support the university's mission of teaching, research, and service.

Our collections include:

- Over 5 million volumes
- 100,000+ electronic journals
- 1.5 million e-books
- Extensive special collections and archives
