## Our Mission

The University of Iowa Libraries advances research, teaching, and learning by providing access to information resources, expert guidance, and innovative services. We are committed to creating inclusive spaces and services that support the diverse needs of our community.

## Special Collections

The Libraries is home to significant research collections:

- **Special Collections & University Archives** - Rare books, manuscripts, and university historical records
- **Iowa Women's Archives** - Documenting the lives and contributions of Iowa women
- **Iowa Digital Library** - Digitized photographs, maps, and historical documents

## Services

We offer a wide range of services to support your research and learning:

- Research consultations with subject librarians
- Course reserves and instructional services
- Interlibrary loan and document delivery
- Technology lending and digital scholarship support
- Study spaces and collaborative areas
